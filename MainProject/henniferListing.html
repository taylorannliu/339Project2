Hennifer

Fancy Brown

A delightful, brown hen, Hennifer enjoys cornmeal, siestas, and belly scritches. She loves humans but does not get along with dogs.

Hennifer is a social chicken who needs to live among at least two other friends to thrive. Her preferred enclosure type is a large coop with plenty of nesting space.

Details

Feeding Schedule

Once a day, 100g of neutral bird feed.

Ideal Temperature

18° - 24°C

Egg Quality

4 large brown eggs per week
