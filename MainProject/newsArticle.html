Vancouver's semi-annual Chicken Run next July

Event

05/28/2023

By Ken Chi

Chickens running amuck, there's

Just kidding it's just Vancouver's semi-annual Chicken Run.

Come to the event to witness the most chaotic and intense chicken race there is all of the Lower Mainland. You not want to miss it. There's games and activities like pin the beak on the chicken as well as feed tastings for your feathery friends. It's a whole family and farm affair, you won't want to miss it. But not only is it for the fun and feathered, there farm friends and welcome to come too! We have pigs derbys, cow milking competitions and more. Make sure you don't miss the farm fresh lunch in and gourmet ice cream. It is a real hoot and a half.
