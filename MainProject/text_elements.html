Colours

Primary Colour #EC5454

This is the major accent colour of the site used to show actions (ex: checkout) or things that are active (ex: navigation bar tab). It is also used as a heading for important info.

Secondary Colour #AAD6FF

This colour is used alongside the primary colour to show contrast and interaction, but be secondary in importance to the primary elements. It is used for buttons that are secondary to another button on the page, like the reset button compared to the submit button.
