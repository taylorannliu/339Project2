Silkie Egg

Medium

A medium-sized oval egg that may hatch a Silkie chicken.

Silkies are playful, sociable chickens known for their beautiful and fluffy coats. Please note that an incubator is required to hatch this egg. Eggs are most likely to, but not guaranteed to hatch.

Hatch Time

Around 22 days

Incubator Temperature

22° - 24°C
