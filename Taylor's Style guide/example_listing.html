About this Chicken

A delightful, brown hen, Hennifer enjoys cornmeal, siestas, and belly scritches. She loves humans but does not get along with dogs.

Hennifer is a social chicken who needs to live among at least 2 other friends to thrive. Her preferred enclosure type is large a large coop with plenty of nesting space.
