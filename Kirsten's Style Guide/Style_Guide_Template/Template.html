What does the company do, or offer?

Fiesty Stockings provides a range of decorative and stylish socks that will upgrade your wardrobe. Their specialty socks aims to create a trend in luxurious-looking socks that will give customers and extra booth of confidence.

Who is the company to potential customers? (as a person)

Potential customers are from teenagers to adults that are interested in fashion in particular, interested in feminine and elegant styles

Why might this company entice someone to purchase a product or service?

This company entices customers to purchase do to their its cohesive branding and interesting and bold designs. As well the ability to style the socks with multiple outfits, will make them very popular with introducing an unexpected fashion item that is usually overlooked.
