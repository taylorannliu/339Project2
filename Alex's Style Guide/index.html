Basic Branding Ideas

What Nosotras does

Nosotras is a multicultural bookstore that specializes in literary works that highlight the cultural diversity in Vancouver. There are books in different languages, books with recipes waiting to be discovered, and picture books about interesting cultures that are not talked about enough in modern day media. We have a brick-and-mortar location in Vancouver, but we also run an e-commerce version of the store to increase access to our curated collection of multicultural books.

Who is Nosotras to potential customer

Nosotras is a haven of multiculutral knowledge waiting to be discovered by potential customers. We provide customers with the means of becoming a well-rounded, thoughtfully-educated individual. It is up to the customers to engage with our books to become more mindful and empathetic members of society.

Why might Nosotras entice someone to purchase a product

Nosotras is a bookstore with a unique mission to engage with and introduce underrepresented cultures to the general public. We aim to peak customers' curosity with our thought-provoking and exciting books. Our ambitious mission to create a more educated and welcoming society requires support from those in favour of our mission. The buying power of our supporters is important to Nosotras as it is an indicator that we should continue running the business because our mission resonates with community members. People who purchase from Nosotras demonstrate a drive to learn—an important quality to have for someone who wants to become knowledgeable regarding multiculturalism.
